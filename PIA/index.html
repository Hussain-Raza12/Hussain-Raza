EASA Restores Certification

The European Union Aviation Safety Agency (EASA) has lifted the ban on Pakistan International Airlines (PIA), allowing it to resume operations in European airspace. The decision follows improvements in safety and compliance measures, marking a significant step for PIA’s international revival and restoring its global connectivity.
